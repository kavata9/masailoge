In Norse mythology, Ragnarök is the battle at the end of the world.

The battle is to be fought between the gods(Æsir) led by Odin; and the fire giants,the Jötnar and other monsters, led by Loki and Surtr

It is harsh in the world,whoredom rife
—an axe age, a sword age—shields are riven—
a wind age, a wolf age—before the world goes headlong.
No man will have mercy on another. (Edda poem;Völuspá)

Battles:

  • Freyr vs Surtr: Freyr will be unarmed, and killed by Surtr.
  • Odin vs Fenrir: Odin will be swallowed by Fenrir.
  • Tyr vs Garm: Both will slay each other.
  • Heimdall vs Loki: Both will inflict fatal wounds on each other,
    but Loki shall refuse to die until he sees the destruction of the worlds
